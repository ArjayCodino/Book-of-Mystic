Past-Present-Future Tarot Card Spread


As a professional tarot card reader with years of experience, I’m thrilled to share with you one of my favorite and most versatile tarot spreads: the Past-Present-Future spread.


This simple yet powerful layout offers insights into the past, present, and future, and can be used for anything from self-reflection to decision-making.

It’s also a great introductory spread for those just beginning their tarot journey. So, grab your favorite deck, and let’s dive into this mystical world of tarot together!

The Basics of a Past-Present-Future Spread


The Past-Present-Future spread is a three-card layout, with each card representing a different time frame.

This spread is perfect for getting an overview of a situation, understanding how past experiences have shaped the present, and gaining insights into the potential outcomes of a decision or event.

It’s also an excellent way to develop your intuition and tarot reading skills.


Preparing for the Reading

Before we dive into the actual reading process, here are a few essential steps to ensure you get the most out of your tarot experience:


Choose a quiet, comfortable space: Tarot readings are best done in a calm environment free from distractions. Find a spot where you can sit comfortably and focus on your cards.

Clear your mind: Take a few deep breaths, close your eyes, and allow any thoughts or worries to float away. The more centered and relaxed you are, the clearer your intuition will be.

Set your intention: Before drawing your cards, take a moment to think about the question or situation you’d like to explore. Be as specific as possible, and remember that tarot is a tool for guidance, not a crystal ball.

Shuffle your deck: Give your cards a good shuffle, ensuring that they are well mixed. While shuffling, continue to focus on your intention and visualize the energy flowing through the cards.

Performing the Past-Present-Future Spread

Once you’ve prepared yourself and your deck, it’s time to lay out your cards. Here’s a step-by-step guide to performing the Past-Present-Future spread:


Draw three cards: With your intention in mind, draw the top three cards from your shuffled deck. Don’t overthink this process; trust your intuition to guide you.

Lay out the cards: Place the cards face-down in front of you, from left to right. The first card represents the past, the second the present, and the third the future.
Turn over the first card: Reveal the past card, which represents past events, emotions, or energies that have influenced your present situation. Take a moment to observe the imagery, symbols, and colors on the card, and consider how they relate to your question or intention.

Turn over the second card: Next, reveal the present card. This card offers insight into your current situation, including any challenges, opportunities, or energies that are at play. Reflect on how this card connects to the past card, and what lessons or guidance it may offer.

Turn over the third card: Finally, reveal the future card, which represents potential outcomes or influences in the future. While the future is never set in stone, this card provides a glimpse into the possible paths that lie ahead, based on your present situation and past experiences.

Reflect on the reading: Take some time to contemplate the story that unfolds across the three cards, and how the messages relate to your question or intention. Remember, tarot is a powerful tool for self-discovery and growth, so consider how the insights from this reading can help you make empowered decisions and embrace the opportunities that come your way.

In Conclusion


The Past-Present-Future tarot spread is a versatile and insightful layout that’s perfect for both beginners and seasoned readers alike.

By exploring the connections between past influences, present circumstances, and future possibilities, this spread offers valuable guidance for navigating life’s challenges and embracing personal growth.
As you continue to practice this spread and deepen your tarot journey, remember that your intuition is your greatest ally.

Trust yourself, and allow the cards to speak to you in their unique language of symbols, imagery, and energy. Happy reading!
